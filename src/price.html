About The Lessons

    • Conversation

    This course is designed to help you achieve English fluency. Lessons focus on accuracy of language, fluency, accent reduction, and language used in daily conversations. With this course you will learn how to speak naturally and without hesitation.


    Test Preparation

    Students can choose to focus on test preparation. I will work together with you to achieve optimal results in English tests such as the TOELF ~TOEIC ~IELTS or GRE exam. Live lessons will focus on the speaking and writing forms of these tests. In addition, I will provide you with activities to improve your listening and reading skills.


    General English

    This course addresses the building blocks of the English language: grammar, vocabulary and pronunciation. It covers a broad range of practice in listening, reading, writing, and speaking.


    English Writing

    The English Writing Course is great for students who want to improve their written communication skills for school or work. It is also specifically aimed for students who are applying to English speaking universities and need assistance with their college applications. Each lesson we will focus on a specific writing tasks related to your area of interest. Example areas can be writing academic essays, college statement of purpose essays, emails, and resumes.
