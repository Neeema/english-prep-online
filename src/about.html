Hi! My name is Amy, and I am a Cambridge CELTA qualified English teacher from the United States.


I started English Prep Live as a way to help anyone in the world get the real, native English they need to achieve their English learning goals. Therefore, if you’re looking to:

  • Become a fluent English speaker
  • Get a high band or score on an international English exam
  • Use English grammar and vocabulary with confidence

    • I am here to help!

    At English Prep Live, I specialize in teaching English conversation, test preparation, general English, and English writing. My live Skype lessons emphasize speaking and writing, and I also use many techniques to tailor my lessons specifically to your needs and learning style.

    At English Prep Live, I teach grammar in context, so you can see how grammar is really used in real-world situations. I also offer customized lesson summaries after each lesson that provide error correction, learning advice and activities to keep you actively using English all throughout the week.

    My background is in conversational English, English writing and test preparation. I’ve specialized in tutoring international students in English grammar and writing at an American university. I also have extensive experience teaching English in Japan where I customized lessons in conversational English and Exam preparation such as TOEFL, IELTS, and EIKEN.


    My goal is to make learning English and studying for English exams fun and stress-free! My lessons involve:


    Variety and Flexibility

    I understand that every student has their own learning style. Therefore my lessons are versatile and flexible. I use a variety of teaching methods and resources from games to media in order to cater to all learning styles.

    Language Immersion

    I find the fastest way to learn a language is by practicing thinking and interacting in that language. My lessons incorporate interactive activities and offer opportunities for lots and lots of speaking practice.

    Creativity and Discovery

    I believe that creativity and discovery are just as important as vocabulary and grammar, as it quickens the pace of learning. As a result, my lessons are fun, interactive and interesting. Each student will be their own explorer of the English language.

    Communicative Meaning

    We remember new material best when we can relate what we learn to our own lives. Therefore my lesson activities and conversation topics will be related to your life and goals.

    Positive Feedback and Continuous Assessment

    Each lesson will be continually assessed by me to guarantee optimal progress. During the class I will make a list of any areas in need of improvement. These areas will then be addressed in your lesson summary.
