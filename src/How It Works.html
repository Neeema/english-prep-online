    • Frequently Asked Questions:


    1. How do I begin?

    You can begin by signing up for a FREE trial lesson via the online scheduler. You can use the message box to talk about your language objectives and goals. Next we will receive an email from Teacher Amy regarding more information about your free lesson!

    2. How do I schedule a class?

    You can schedule your lesson(s) in your time zone through the user-friendly online student scheduler. Scheduling is flexible. Lessons can be purchased in sets of 1, 5 and 10. If you purchase a 5 or 10 set lesson package, you can book all your lessons in advance at the time of purchase on the student scheduler, or you can schedule your lessons one at a time via email. Whichever way is most convenient for you!

    3. How do I purchase my lesson(s)?

    All lessons and lesson packages are purchased via PayPal or credit card.

    4. What if I need to cancel a lesson?

    You can cancel a lesson up to 48 hours before your scheduled time. In doing so you have the opportunity to reschedule your lesson, which can be done by email. Any lessons cancelled after this period will be lost.

    5. What if I am late for class?

    In the event that you may be late, please notify Teacher Amy by way of email or Skype IM. Your scheduled time-slot will be kept on reserve for your entire lesson hour, so no worries if you are late! If you do not show-up for your lesson and have not given prior notice, your lesson will be lost.

    6. Do lesson packages have a time-frame?

    Lesson packages are designed to ensure optimal progress with positive results. Consistency is key to improving your language skills. Therefore lesson packages must be completed within the time-frame given.
    -5 hour lesson package: completed in 7 weeks.
    -10 hour lesson package: completed in 12 weeks.
    * It is possible to extend a lesson package past the time-frame under certain circumstances and at the discretion of Teacher Amy. These circumstances can be for any unforeseen reason such as illness or an unexpected family/work related commitment.

    7. What if I would like to cancel a lesson package?

    You may cancel a lesson package for any reason within the first two lessons, and you will receive a full refund on your remaining lessons. If you decide you would like to cancel your lesson package after your first two lessons, you may do so with a 10% administrative charge levied from your remaining lessons.
